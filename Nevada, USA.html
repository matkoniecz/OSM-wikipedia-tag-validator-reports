wikipedia tag links to 404


Wikipedia article linked from OSM object using wikipedia tag is missing. Often article was moved without leaving redirect and wikipedia tag should be edited to point to the new one. Article may be deleted and no longer existing, or link was never valid. In such cases wikipedia tag should be deleted.
